Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order value you work with?
We work with clients across a broad range of order sizes — from under $1,000 for individual importers to $150,000+ for wholesale and commercial orders. There is no strict minimum; we tailor our service to your budget and scale.
How do you handle customs and import duties?
We provide full customs documentation support including commercial invoices, packing lists, certificates of origin, and HS code classification. While import duties are the client's legal responsibility at the destination country, we advise on expected costs and work with trusted customs brokers in Ghana and Canada to ensure smooth clearance.
What is the typical shipping time from China to Ghana or Canada?
Sea freight from China to Ghana typically takes 25–35 days. China to Canada runs 20–30 days by sea. Air freight options are available for time-sensitive shipments and can cut transit time to 5–10 days. We provide estimated timelines in every sourcing proposal.
Can I get product samples before placing a bulk order?
Absolutely — and we strongly recommend it. We arrange samples directly from shortlisted suppliers, coordinate shipping to your location, and advise on quality benchmarks. Sample costs are typically borne by the client and can be credited toward a confirmed bulk order.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers (SWIFT/wire), mobile money (for Ghana-based clients), and other methods agreed upon in the service contract. Full payment terms are outlined in your formal service agreement before any order is placed.
Do you offer private label and OEM manufacturing?
Yes. We work with verified factories across China that offer private label and OEM services for a wide range of product categories including cosmetics, apparel, electronics accessories, and packaged goods. Minimum quantities vary by factory and product type.
How quickly will I receive a quote after submitting a request?
We respond to all sourcing requests within 24–48 business hours with a tailored proposal or a follow-up call to clarify requirements. Complex or high-volume requests may require up to 72 hours for a comprehensive response.
